# Research: Org-Scale Agentic Governance

## Inputs
- Anthropic scaling guide benchmark PDF.
- Existing Joyus governance docs and feature lifecycle artifacts.

## R1: Adoption Model
- Decision: Use pilot plus champion plus enablement cadence pattern.
- Why: balances speed with knowledge transfer and consistency.

## R2: ROI Contract
- Decision: Require named ownership and review cadence for baseline and ongoing metrics.
- Why: avoids anecdotal reporting and improves decision quality.

## R3: MCP Governance Lifecycle
- Decision: Define approval rubric and quarterly audit model.
- Why: supports controlled expansion without blocking innovation.

## R4: Enforcement Mechanism
- Decision: Implement governance checks in script and CI, plus pride-status surfacing.
- Why: policy without tooling is non-durable.
